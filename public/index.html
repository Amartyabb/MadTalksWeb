Mission

MADTalks was created to spark exploration. In our talks, we hope to bring together the diverse perspectives of leaders in a wide range of fields to encourage debate in these areas, which we strongly believe can spur the development of new ideas and change ways of thinking.

What is MADTalks?

MADTalks is an independently organized speaker series at James Madison High School in northern Virginia. A platform for conversation and analysis, MADTalks fosters discussion about trends in diverse fields—from neuroscience to capital markets to cloud computing—through live 30-minute interview-style talks. MADTalks also gives high school students the unique opportunity to meet leaders in these fields and learn from their expertise.

THE STATE AND FUTURE OF CLOUD COMPUTING: November 15, 2017

About The Talk

Jessica Tavasti Davis has seen the rapid growth and change of cloud computing within the government in the last decade. In spite of projections that the cloud market will top $400 billion by 2020, Davis argues that there still remains a key challenge: how people perceive the cloud. She makes the case that misconceptions about privacy and security are the main blocker for organizations to transition entirely to the cloud.

Jessica Davis

Jessica Tavasti Davis has worked at Microsoft for over nine years, and she brings extensive expertise from a variety of roles and projects in the cloud field. She is currently Director of Business Applications for the US Public Sector, where she works with federal, state, and local governments to develop and implement Microsoft’s cloud solutions that enhance productivity, increase return on investment, and reduce costs.

Marketing the Burrito Bowl: September 27, 2017

About The Talk

Every day, about one million people eat Chipotle. Jake Park takes us inside the fast-casual restaurant’s unconventional philosophy of reaching and retaining customers and its “advertising as education” approach to marketing. He also discusses the defined meaning of Chipotle’s brand promise after several catastrophic food safety crises.

Jake Park

Jake Park helps design the framework necessary for Chipotle to reach its customers and expand its market both locally and nationally. Previously, he was Senior Brand Associate for Capital One, working specializing in small businesses.
